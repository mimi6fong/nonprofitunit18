Austin Marathon

Sunday, February 20th, 2022
7:00am
Downtown Austin

Registration for the 2022 Austin Marathon & Half Marathon and 5K is now open and we want YOU to join our Texas 4000 Team! We are so excited that the Austin Gives Miles Program invited us to be a returning charity and we look forward to surpassing last year’s goal. We’re aiming high again this year and hope to raise $10,000 to further our mission to cultivate student leaders and engage communities in the fight against cancer! Click here to register for the Marathon, Half Marathon, or 5K and click here to join our team in the fight against cancer! We’ve got some great fundraising incentives, including: Pedal Pal ($250.00 – $399.00) Fundraise more than $250 and receive a Texas 4000 dri-fit shirt and hat! Paceline Partner ($400.00 – $999.00) Fundraise up to $999 and receive a Texas 4000 team jersey, dri-fit shirt and hat! Century Supporter ($1,000.00 – $2,499.00) Fundraise up to $2,499 and receive a Texas 4000 team jersey, dri-fit shirt, and hat. Super SAG ($2,500.00 + ) Fundraise more than $2,500 and receive a Texas 4000 team jersey, dri-fit shirt, and hat, and 2 tickets to the 2022 Tribute Gala. The Tribute Gala is a fundraising event to help support the next Texas 4000 team and is also a the welcome home event that celebrates the most recent group of riders to reach Alaska.
